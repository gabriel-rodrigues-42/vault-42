# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **Gardening enthusiasts** — people who love cultivating plants at home, tracking growth, and seeking rare or heirloom seeds. They care about high-quality botanical imagery, detailed care instructions, and seed purity.
- **Professional horticulturists** — commercial growers, landscapers, and nursery managers looking for reliable germination details, exact scientific names, and bulk/wholesale capability.

**Context of use:** Mobile-first while in the garden or greenhouse checking details, and desktop for research and large-scale cart orders.

## Product Purpose

Vault 42 is a premium, specialized seeds and plants e-commerce shop. It exists to make discovering, researching, and purchasing plants and seeds a clean, visual, and highly informative experience.

**What success looks like:** A customer finds a specific heirloom seed or exotic plant, gets clear specifications (soil, sun, watering, exact species name), adds it to their cart, and checks out with confidence, feeling that Vault 42 is an expert, top-tier botanical purveyor.

## Brand Personality

**3 words:** Organic · Premium · Expert

**Voice:** Professional, clean, and knowledgeable. Speaks with authority on plant care and botanical facts without jargon overload.

**Tone spectrum:**
- Encouraging and natural during navigation
- Meticulous and specific in product detail sheets
- Direct and clean during checkout
- Calm and helpful in empty/error states

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS-cream / pastel-heavy startup interfaces** — No cartoon doodles, flat clip-art illustrations, or low-contrast warm beige gradients.
- **Cluttered "discount-store" e-commerce templates** — No aggressive discount countdowns, fake urgency badges, or cluttered side-stripes.
- **Vague descriptions** — No generic filler text or missing botanical details (e.g. soil/sun requirements).

## Design Principles

1. **Botanical Precision** — Every product listing must show factual cultivation info (sunlight, soil, watering, species) in a structured, readable way.
2. **Natural High Contrast** — Deep forest greens paired with clean white/light surfaces, or deep dark theme mode elements. Text contrast must be impeccable.
3. **Product as the Hero** — Let high-quality photos of the leaves, seeds, and flowers be the primary visual elements. Keep cards clean, borders thin, and padding generous.
4. **Frictionless Utility** — Fast search, clear cart state, and a clean, direct checkout flow. No hidden fees or dark patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG AA contrast target**: Minimum contrast of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for headers.
- **iOS Zoom Prevention**: Form inputs must have at least 16px font size.
- **Semantic Structure**: Screen-reader accessible headings and descriptive image alt tags for botanical items.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect prefers-reduced-motion media query.
